## CONCEPT B-2 — "She Walked In"

- **Feeling:** empowerment / "main character entered"
- **Hairstyle (this concept):** long straight jet-black curtain hair, center part, ends brushing the elbows, glass-shine
- **Magic move:** every surface and object in a café **turns to face her** the moment she enters — chairs pivot, flowers lean, lights brighten toward her — then snap back to normal when she sits
- **Concept line:** "she walked in"
- **Continuous shot:** one unbroken move through a busy artisan café, 6 frames
- **Loop:** final frame returns everything to its neutral pre-entrance state
