# AD 1 — FACE SERUM · "The Glow Proof" (demo / before-after-in-one-take, ~25s)
PRODUCT: [BRAND] [Vitamin-C / Glow Serum].
BIG IDEA: don't TELL them it works — show visible glow happen on bare skin in real time, in one honest, no-filter close-up.
TARGET FEELING: trust + desire ("I want THAT skin").
LENGTH: ~25s.
STRUCTURE:
- [0–3s] HOOK · SHOT: extreme macro on Aira's bare, un-made-up cheek in clean daylight, dewy but real (pores visible). COPY (VO, warm): "This is my skin with zero makeup. Watch what three drops do." SUPER: "no filter. no foundation."
- [3–8s] PROBLEM · SHOT: she looks at her skin in a mirror, neutral, points to a dull/tired area under-eye. VO: "Dull, tired, a bit uneven — winter skin, basically." SUPER: "dull · tired · uneven".
- [8–15s] PRODUCT HERO · SHOT: glossy macro of the dropper releasing three golden drops into her palm; slow product-beauty shot, bottle catching light. VO: "Three drops. Vitamin C, niacinamide, hyaluronic acid." SUPER: "the [PRODUCT] ✦".
- [15–20s] PROOF/DEMO · SHOT: she presses it in; a clean push-in on the cheek as the skin visibly drinks it and starts to glow — dewy, plumped, luminous (real, not CGI). VO, a little wow: "Look at that. It just... drinks it." SUPER: "watch the glow →".
- [20–25s] PAYOFF + CTA · SHOT: she turns to lens, fresh radiant bare-skin smile, holds the bottle by her cheek. VO: "Skin that looks like skin — just better. This is the one." SUPER + CTA: "[BRAND] Glow Serum · link in bio · code AIRA15".
HERO CLAIMS (placeholder): "visibly brighter in [X] days," "72-hour hydration," "dermatologist-tested," "non-sticky."
CTA: "Get the glow → link in bio. Use AIRA15. Your future skin says thank you."
CAPTION: "#ad I don't post products I don't actually use, and this serum earned its spot on my shelf. Three drops, real glow, no filter needed. 🍊✨ Linked it for you — code AIRA15. #skincare #glowserum #vitaminc"
PRODUCTION NOTES: shoot in soft north daylight, NO beauty-filter (authenticity is the whole sell); real macro lens; the "glow" should be lighting + genuine product dewiness, not post FX.

# AD 2 — LIPSTICK · "The Torture Test" (torture-test / proof-driven, ~22s)
PRODUCT: [BRAND] [Transfer-Proof Matte Lip].
BIG IDEA: prove the "won't budge" claim by trying (and failing) to ruin it — coffee, kissing a mug, a burger, a white shirt. Fun, confident, evidence-based.
TARGET FEELING: delight + "okay I'm convinced."
LENGTH: ~22s.
STRUCTURE:
- [0–3s] HOOK · SHOT: Aira applies the lip once, fast, then looks at lens with a challenging grin. COPY (VO, playful): "They said transfer-proof. Let's actually test that." SUPER: "transfer-proof?? let's see. 👀".
- [3–8s] TEST 1 · SHOT: close-up — she sips from a white coffee mug, pulls it away → mug rim CLEAN. VO: "Coffee. Clean." SUPER: "test 1: ☕ PASS".
- [8–12s] TEST 2 · SHOT: she presses her lips to the back of her hand → no mark. VO, eyebrows up: "Okay, the hand test. Nothing." SUPER: "test 2: 💋 PASS".
- [12–16s] TEST 3 · SHOT: she takes a big bite of a messy burger, chews, grins → lip still perfect. VO, laughing: "A whole BURGER. Are you kidding me." SUPER: "test 3: 🍔 PASS".
- [16–20s] PRODUCT HERO · SHOT: glossy bullet-twist macro of the lipstick, swatch of the shade, name. VO: "Eight hours. One coat. Doesn't budge." SUPER: "[PRODUCT] in 'shade name'".
- [20–22s] PAYOFF + CTA · SHOT: she blows a kiss to lens, lip flawless. VO: "Believe the hype." SUPER + CTA: "shop now · link in bio".
HERO CLAIMS (placeholder): "8-hour wear," "transfer-proof," "one-swipe coverage," "non-drying matte."
CTA: "The shade is 'X' and it's linked in my bio. Run, don't walk. 💋"
CAPTION: "#ad I tried to ruin this lipstick and it WOULD NOT let me 😭 coffee, a burger, everything — still perfect. Obsessed. Shade linked in bio! 💋 #lipstick #transferproof #makeup #beautytok"
PRODUCTION NOTES: real demos, single takes where possible (the realism is the proof); crisp macro on each "clean" reveal; upbeat playful track; quick satisfying cuts and SUPER stamps ("PASS") for shareability.

# AD 3 — FRAGRANCE · "Bottled Golden Hour" (cinematic / sensory, ~30s)
PRODUCT: [BRAND] [Eau de Parfum].
BIG IDEA: you can't smell a scent through a screen — so SELL THE FEELING. A wordless cinematic mood-film that makes the fragrance feel like a warm golden-hour memory. Aspirational, sensory, high-end.
TARGET FEELING: longing + luxury ("I want to feel like that").
LENGTH: ~30s.
STRUCTURE (mostly visual + a few whispered lines / end-line):
- [0–5s] HOOK · SHOT: slow-motion macro — golden sunlight through sheer curtains, Aira's silhouette turning, hair catching light; dreamy, warm. SUPER: (none yet — let it breathe). VO (soft, intimate whisper): "Some feelings deserve a scent."
- [5–12s] MOOD BUILD · SHOT: a montage of warm sensory fragments — fingertips trailing a silk slip-dress, a wrist lifted near the jaw, sun-flare, a soft genuine smile, linen and skin. Slow, luxurious, gold. VO: (silence — music swells).
- [12–18s] PRODUCT HERO · SHOT: the bottle on a sunlit surface, light refracting through the liquid (amber-gold), a hand reaching for it in slow motion, a single elegant spritz catching the light as mist. SUPER: "[PRODUCT] · Eau de Parfum".
- [18–24s] THE FEELING · SHOT: Aira, freshly spritzed, eyes closing as she breathes it in, a slow content exhale, then opening her eyes warm and luminous to the low sun. VO (soft): "Warm. A little gold. Unforgettable." SUPER: "notes: amber · vanilla · neroli".
- [24–30s] PAYOFF + CTA · SHOT: she walks into the golden light away from camera, glancing back once, the bottle held loosely; logo resolve over warm bokeh. VO: "Wear the way it feels." SUPER + CTA: "[BRAND] · discover the scent · link in bio".
HERO CLAIMS (placeholder): "long-lasting eau de parfum," "warm amber-vanilla," "for day into night."
CTA: "Some things you have to feel. Discover [PRODUCT] → link in bio. 🌅"
CAPTION: "#ad This is what golden hour would smell like if you could bottle it. Warm amber, soft vanilla, a little bit of magic. My new signature. 🌅 Linked in bio. #fragrance #perfume #eaudeparfum"
PRODUCTION NOTES: shoot at real golden hour or warm-gel equivalent; slow-motion + shallow macro; almost no dialogue (fragrance = mood, not facts); lush orchestral/ambient swell carries it; ultra-premium colour grade, maximum warm-gold but rich (not washed).

# AD 4 — HAIRCARE · "From Frizz to Glass" (transformation-proof / split-screen, ~24s)
PRODUCT: [BRAND] [Smoothing Hair Serum / Mask].
BIG IDEA: an honest, satisfying before-and-after — dry, frizzy, dull "before" vs glassy, smooth, shiny "after" — with the satisfying application moment as the centrepiece. Proof you can SEE.
TARGET FEELING: oddly-satisfying + "my hair NEEDS this."
LENGTH: ~24s.
STRUCTURE:
- [0–3s] HOOK · SHOT: split-screen / quick toggle — her frizzy dry hair on one side, glass-smooth on the other. COPY (VO): "Same hair. Same day. One product." SUPER: "frizz → glass ✨".
- [3–9s] PROBLEM · SHOT: real close-up of dry, frizzy, flyaway ends in unflattering honest light; she tugs a brush through and it catches. VO, relatable: "Humidity destroys me. Frizz, flyaways, zero shine — by noon it's over." SUPER: "the frizz struggle 😩".
- [9–15s] PRODUCT HERO + APPLICATION · SHOT: pumps the serum into palms (glossy macro), smooths it down the lengths — the ASMR-satisfying glide, hair instantly laying down sleek. VO: "Two pumps, mid-length to ends. Watch it." SUPER: "[PRODUCT] · 2 pumps".
- [15–20s] PROOF · SHOT: slow reveal — she flips the now glass-smooth, mirror-shiny hair; light glides along it; she runs fingers through with zero snag. VO, satisfied: "Glass. No frizz, all shine, and it's not greasy." SUPER: "💧 no grease · all shine".
- [20–24s] PAYOFF + CTA · SHOT: confident hair-flip to lens, glossy smile. VO: "This is the one that actually works." SUPER + CTA: "shop [BRAND] · link in bio · AIRA10".
HERO CLAIMS (placeholder): "humidity-resistant smoothing," "adds shine," "heat-protectant," "lightweight, non-greasy."
CTA: "Frizz-free, glass-shine hair → it's linked in my bio. Code AIRA10. 💧"
CAPTION: "#ad From frizz to GLASS in two pumps 😮‍💨 I've tried everything and this is the only thing that survives humidity. Linked it + code AIRA10. #haircare #frizzcontrol #glasshair #hairtok"
PRODUCTION NOTES: keep the 'before' genuinely unflattering (honesty sells the after); macro on the smoothing glide (the satisfying hero moment); consistent lighting before/after so the change reads as real; crisp shine highlights; satisfying ASMR audio of the glide + a clean beat.

# AD 5 — SPF / SKINCARE · "The Invisible Step You're Skipping" (problem-solution / education, ~26s)
PRODUCT: [BRAND] [Daily SPF 50 Fluid].
BIG IDEA: confront the #1 objection ("SPF is greasy, white, a pain") and dismantle it — an educational, no-BS problem-solution ad that positions this SPF as the one that fixed all of it. Smart, trustworthy, slightly myth-busting.
TARGET FEELING: "oh, I've been doing this wrong" + relief.
LENGTH: ~26s.
STRUCTURE:
- [0–4s] HOOK · SHOT: Aira to lens, finger up, honest — close, bright daylight, fresh skin. COPY (VO): "If your skincare doesn't end with SPF, you're basically wasting it. But I get why you skip it — so did I." SUPER: "the step you're skipping ☀️".
- [4–10s] PROBLEM · SHOT: quick relatable cuts — a thick white cream leaving a grey cast, a greasy forehead, glasses sliding. VO: "Greasy. White cast. Stings my eyes. Pills under makeup. We've all been there." SUPER: "greasy · white cast · pilling 🚫".
- [10–16s] PRODUCT HERO + DEMO · SHOT: she dispenses the lightweight fluid, smooths it on → it MELTS in clear, no white cast (show on bare skin), instant soft glow. VO: "This one is a fluid. Watch — zero white cast, not greasy, gone in seconds." SUPER: "[PRODUCT] SPF 50 · invisible finish".
- [16–22s] PROOF · SHOT: she applies makeup right over it (no pilling), then checks skin in daylight — fresh, glowy, protected. VO: "Sits perfect under makeup. Actually feels like skincare." SUPER: "✓ no cast ✓ no grease ✓ makeup-friendly".
- [22–26s] PAYOFF + CTA · SHOT: confident to lens, tapping cheek, glowing. VO: "Wear it every single day. Future-you is begging you." SUPER + CTA: "[BRAND] SPF 50 · link in bio".
HERO CLAIMS (placeholder): "SPF 50 / broad spectrum," "no white cast," "lightweight fluid," "layers under makeup."
CTA: "Stop skipping it. The SPF that changed my mind → link in bio. ☀️ Your skin in 10 years will thank you."
CAPTION: "#ad The reason you hate SPF is you haven't tried THIS one. No white cast, no grease, disappears into skin, sits under makeup. Daily SPF is the cheapest anti-aging there is. ☀️ Linked! #spf #suncare #skincaretips #antiaging"
PRODUCTION NOTES: educational tone, fast informative cuts; the no-white-cast demo MUST be on bare visible skin in honest daylight (that's the proof); clean bright grade; confident best-friend delivery; end on a trustworthy direct-to-lens.
