## CONCEPT B-5 — "Confidence Is Contagious"

- **Feeling:** empowerment / hype
- **Hairstyle (this concept):** a bold high-volume blowout, face-framing curtain bangs, glossy jet-black, bouncy and full
- **Magic move:** as she walks down a street feeling herself, **the whole city dresses up around her** — buildings gain neon trim, puddles become mirrors, streetlights bloom into chandeliers, grey pedestrians gain color — her energy infects everything
- **Concept line:** "I didn't change the city — the city changed for me"
- **Continuous shot:** one unbroken tracking shot down an evening city sidewalk, 7 frames
- **Loop:** final frame returns the street to its neutral grey pre-walk state
