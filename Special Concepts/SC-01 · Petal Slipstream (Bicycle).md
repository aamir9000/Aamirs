# AIRA — SC-01 · PETAL SLIPSTREAM (Bicycle) · INGREDIENT MODE (Flow / Veo 3.1) · 9:16 · SILENT
**4 shots × 6s (≈24s reel) · ingredient-native · powerful, not "just riding" · bright colourful daytime**

**Logline:** On a sun-drenched, colourful boulevard Aira launches a sleek bicycle and the ride *earns* its
drama — a coiled push-off, a streaking slipstream, a hard carving lean where her wake blooms into fresh
blossom petals and bright sunlit light, and a commanding hero arrival. One continuous being, four distinct
beats, a single grounded-surreal signature (the bloom), loops back to the opening.

**Arc (why it's engaging, not monotonous):** LAUNCH → SLIPSTREAM → CARVE (bloom apex) → ARRIVAL.
Every shot changes crop, angle and camera move; she is never just pedaling on and on.

## LOOK & RULES
- **Time & light:** **bright daytime** — clear blue sky, clean high natural sun, crisp sunshine with dappled
  light through fresh green leaves and real sun-sparkle on chrome and spokes. High-key and airy.
- **Palette (controlled vibrancy — colourful but curated):** sunlit cream/white facades as the bright base
  + sky-blue + a few **bold curated accents** (a red awning, magenta flower-boxes, a teal door) + the
  blossom-pink wake; anchored by tree-shade greens and neutral stone so colour has depth. Vibrancy comes
  from **bright sun contrast, clean colour-blocking and speculars — NOT blanket oversaturation.** Protect
  natural skin tone — never push saturation onto the face.
- **Believability layer:** 100% real bicycle dynamics — weight transfer, tyre grip/compression in the lean,
  real air-drag on hair/fabric, real petal weight. The one magical beat (the bloom) is grounded in real
  spin/air physics: fresh blossom petals + clean sun speculars and a soft prism shimmer, NOT cartoon
  sparkle, never floaty/weightless, never rainbow-mud.
- **Motion:** real-time natural speed, NO slow-motion. Every clip starts already-in-motion.
- **Identity (constant) vs pose (free):** keep her face/build/hair a fixed skeleton — only gaze/head/soft
  expression animate. But do **NOT** freeze her body — her pose and behaviour vary naturally every beat.
- **Candid, alive, never a posed hold:** real-rider life throughout — glancing around, rising onto the
  pedals then sitting back, one-hand and brief no-hands coasting, brushing a strand of hair from her face,
  a breath / laugh / grin, resetting her grip, shifting weight into the lean. Different natural actions in
  every shot; she reads as a real person riding, not a model holding a pose.
- **Audio (silent reel):** bright, driving cinematic score + diegetic wheel-whir, breeze, birdsong, a soft
  tonal swell on the bloom. (Optional single finale line — left out by default for pure visual power.)

## INGREDIENTS (Ingredients-to-Video — 3 reference images per shot; essence-continuation with CUTS, not morph)
Each shot is a **new camera setup** (angle/size/move/perspective); we **cut** between them and the
3 ingredients hold consistency across the cut. Generate all three per shot in Nano Banana Pro:
- **SUBJECT** — Aira in *this shot's* pose & angle (from her identity-lock reference) → identity + wardrobe.
- **WORLD** — the environment for this beat, no subject → location + light.
- **COMPOSITE / look anchor** — the framed look of this beat → palette + composition.
> Load all three as Ingredients. Continuity = same Aira/world/light across shots + the action momentum
> carried across each cut (match-on-action). We do NOT chain end→start frames (that morphs, not cuts).
