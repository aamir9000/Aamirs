# AIRA — SC-01 · GOLDEN SLIPSTREAM (Bicycle) · INGREDIENT MODE (Flow / Veo 3.1) · 9:16 · SILENT
**4 shots × 6s (≈24s reel) · ingredient-native · powerful, not "just riding"**

**Logline:** Aira launches a sleek bicycle down a golden-hour avenue and the ride *earns* its drama —
a coiled push-off, a streaking slipstream, a hard carving lean where her wake blooms into molten-gold
light and real autumn petals, and a commanding hero arrival. One continuous being, four distinct beats,
a single grounded-surreal signature (the bloom), loops back to the opening.

**Arc (why it's engaging, not monotonous):** LAUNCH → SLIPSTREAM → CARVE (bloom apex) → ARRIVAL.
Every shot changes crop, angle and camera move; she is never just pedaling on and on.

## LOOK & RULES
- **Palette (controlled vibrancy):** warm gold dominant + deep shadow ground for the light to pop +
  one cool accent (dusk-blue sky). Protect natural skin tone — never push saturation onto the face.
- **Believability layer:** 100% real bicycle dynamics — weight transfer, tyre grip/compression in the
  lean, real air-drag on hair/fabric, real petal weight. The one magical beat (the bloom) is grounded in
  real spin/air physics: molten-gold speculars + glow, NOT cartoon sparkle, never floaty/weightless.
- **Motion:** real-time natural speed, NO slow-motion. Every clip starts already-in-motion.
- **Identity:** treat Aira's face/build/hair as a fixed skeleton — only gaze/head/soft expression move.
- **Audio (silent reel):** driving cinematic score + diegetic wheel-whir, wind, a soft tonal swell on the
  bloom. (Optional single finale line if you want one — left out by default for pure visual power.)

## INGREDIENTS (Ingredients-to-Video — 3 reference images per shot; essence-continuation with CUTS, not morph)
Each shot is a **new camera setup** (angle/size/move/perspective); we **cut** between them and the
3 ingredients hold consistency across the cut. Generate all three per shot in Nano Banana Pro:
- **SUBJECT** — Aira in *this shot's* pose & angle (from her identity-lock reference) → identity + wardrobe.
- **WORLD** — the environment for this beat, no subject → location + light.
- **COMPOSITE / look anchor** — the framed look of this beat → palette + composition.
> Load all three as Ingredients. Continuity = same Aira/world/light across shots + the action momentum
> carried across each cut (match-on-action). We do NOT chain end→start frames (that morphs, not cuts).
