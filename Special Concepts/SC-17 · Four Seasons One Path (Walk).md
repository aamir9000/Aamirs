# AIRA — SC-17 · FOUR SEASONS, ONE PATH (Walk) · INGREDIENT MODE (Flow / Veo 3.1) · 9:16 · SILENT · 5 shots × 6s
*Rules & method: see `_INGREDIENT_MODE_README.md`.*

**Logline:** One tree-lined path, walked once — but the world re-skins around her through the four seasons as she
goes: spring blossom, summer green, autumn gold, winter snow, then back to a first bud of spring. The same path,
the same walk; only the season changes. Poetic, magical, continuous. Signature: **a clean season-transition sweep
at each cut — the path's leaves, light and ground morph spring→summer→autumn→winter as the season passes over
her** (season-shift led). Light shifts per season — soft spring → bright summer → warm autumn → cool blue winter.

**Arc:** SPRING BLOSSOM → SUMMER GREEN → AUTUMN GOLD → WINTER SNOW → FIRST SPRING BUD (loop).

## GLOBAL LOCKS
- **WARDROBE:** a **cute layered look that works across seasons** — a cream pleated midi-dress under a colour-block cardigan (sage / blush / rust panels), worn open; a soft scarf and ankle boots she adjusts per season (loose in spring, off-shoulder in summer, wrapped in autumn, bundled with mittens in winter). The base dress + cardigan stay constant; layering changes naturally. **HANDS & NAILS:** short clean nails in soft blush.
- **HAIR:** **long loose natural hair with a soft half-twist**, constant throughout.
- **MAKEUP:** soft natural glow — luminous skin, soft rose lip, fresh per season (dewy spring → sun-warm summer → warm autumn → cool-flushed winter), identity constant.
- **HERO PROP:** the **path itself** (an avenue of arching trees); a single leaf/blossom/seed she catches each season.
- **SIGNATURE (grounded):** at each cut the season sweeps over the path — blossom to green leaves to gold-fall to bare-snow — as a believable seasonal change of the same trees/ground/light, not a cartoon wipe. Real falling blossom, leaves, and snow.
- **JOURNEY + LIGHT (one path, four seasons):** soft pink spring morning → bright green summer noon → warm gold autumn afternoon → cool blue winter dusk → first soft spring light again.
- **INGREDIENTS each shot:** `@subject` (walking the path, isolated, season-lit to match) · `@world` (the same tree path re-skinned to that season, no subject) · `@look` (that season's palette swatch).
- *Footing note: she walks the same path the whole time — natural confident strolling, weight planted on the path; only the season around her changes.*
