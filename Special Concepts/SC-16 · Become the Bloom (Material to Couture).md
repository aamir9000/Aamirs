# AIRA — SC-16 · BECOME THE BLOOM (Material → Couture) · INGREDIENT MODE (Flow / Veo 3.1) · 9:16 · SILENT · 3 shots × 6s
*Rules & method: see `_INGREDIENT_MODE_README.md`. NOTE: this is a fusion concept — the WARDROBE deliberately
**transforms** across shots (that IS the signature), so the "constant wardrobe" rule is the intentional exception here.*

**Logline:** The most ingredient-native concept: a **material becomes couture**. The subject walks a luxe garden
path in a simple slip; a drift of fresh blossom petals and soft light climbs her form and, beat by beat, **builds
itself into a full haute-couture gown** — grounded (real petal weight, real fabric drape forming), never cartoon.
Three beats: the gathering, the forming, the bloom-complete reveal.

**Arc:** GATHERING (garden path) → FORMING (marble colonnade) → BLOOM-COMPLETE (fountain court / hero plinth).

- **HAIR:** a soft sculptural **low chignon** with a few loose tendrils.
