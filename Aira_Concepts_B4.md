## CONCEPT B-4 — "Don't Text Him Back"

- **Feeling:** deeply relatable / inner conflict humor
- **Hairstyle (this concept):** a loose tousled French twist, pieces falling around the face deliberately, a single decorative gold hairpin
- **Magic move:** her phone **physically resists her** — it slides away from her reaching hand, the screen dims when she picks it up, the keyboard letters scramble — because even the world knows she shouldn't text back
- **Concept line:** "even my phone said no"
- **Continuous shot:** one unbroken move on a sofa in a cozy apartment, 6 frames
- **Loop:** final frame returns to the exact opening pose with the phone buzzing again
