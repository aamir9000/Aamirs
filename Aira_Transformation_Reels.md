## SAFETY NOTE (read)
These transformations turn Aira into ELEMENTS, MATERIALS, CREATURES and STYLE-ARCHETYPES — never into a real, identifiable living person. A "turn into [a named real person]" morph is impersonation of a real individual and is intentionally NOT included. Where a real-person look was requested, it is replaced with an ORIGINAL archetype (e.g. the "K-drama heroine" aesthetic in C-5) — a styling/mood, not anyone's specific face.
