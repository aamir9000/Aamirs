**20-WORD REEL SUMMARY:** Aira steps into sudden warm summer rain on cobblestone, lifts face skyward, laughs open-mouth, mists perfume, seals with golden flying-kiss.

**WORLD / THEME:** Old European cobblestone alley caught in a sudden warm summer downpour at golden hour. Romantic, weather-broken, sovereign.

**HERO PRODUCT:** Square-cut crystal perfume bottle, faceted glass with a brushed-gold collar and a heavy gold-capped atomizer, warm amber liquid inside catching light. Supporting piece — a folded ivory silk-blend trench coat draped over forearm.

**LIGHTING MOOD:** Warm golden-hour direct sun (camera-right, low-angle, breaking through rain) as dominant key + cool sky bounce (camera-left, soft, diffused by rain veil) as secondary.

**HOOK DISTRIBUTION:** Frame 1 — environmental reveal hook (sudden rain breaking on stone with subject already centered). Frame 3 — direct eye-contact lock through rain droplets on lashes. Frame 5 — perfume mist macro release. Frame 7 — flying-kiss lens release as payoff.
