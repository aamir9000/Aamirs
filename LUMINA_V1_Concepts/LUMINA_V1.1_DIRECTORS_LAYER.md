# LUMINA V1.1 — DIRECTOR'S LAYER UPGRADE

This addendum upgrades every concept from spec-compliant to elite-tier. It defines the 15 craft elements that every frame must now carry in addition to the V1.0 protocol. The Director's Layer is appended to every concept after the existing frames, structured frame-by-frame.

---

## THE 15 DIRECTOR'S LAYER ELEMENTS

### 1. SOUND DESIGN POETRY
Every frame names: one diegetic anchor sound (the single sound that pins the world), one negative-space silence beat (the held quiet), one foley specificity line (named texture-sound), and where applicable an ASMR-grade intimacy marker for macro frames.

### 2. TIMING MICRO-CHOREOGRAPHY
Every VEO is mapped to a four-beat 8-second timeline:
- 0:00–0:02 entry beat
- 0:02–0:04 gesture build
- 0:04–0:06 hook trigger
- 0:06–0:08 release/seal

### 3. GAZE PHYSICS
Every frame names: gaze entry point, gaze travel arc, gaze hold duration in seconds or beats, pupil dilation cue (warm catchlight reaction), and lash blink count for the 8-second window.

### 4. BREATH CHOREOGRAPHY
Every frame names: inhale start, hold beat, exhale release, where the chest rises in frame, and any audible breath markers in the audio layer.

### 5. MICRO-SKIN STORYTELLING
Every frame names at least one of: goosebump beat, capillary flush point, throat-pulse heartbeat visibility, breath fog or breath warmth contact moment, moisture migration path.

### 6. PRODUCT INTERACTION SOUND-FOLEY
Every frame in which product is touched, lifted, set down, or applied names the specific sound the contact makes (cap thock, glass-on-walnut ring, doe-foot kiss-release, magnetic snap).

### 7. WARDROBE INTERIOR LIFE
Every frame names at least one of: bias-seam tension shift with breath, fabric memory fold, strap micro-migration in millimeters, hem weight physics, garment-skin contact friction.

### 8. ENVIRONMENTAL LIFE MARKER
Every concept assigns one living thing in the world doing its own thing across the reel: candle flame rhythm, steam pattern, dust-mote travel, condensation drip rhythm, curtain breath, distant headlight pass.

### 9. COLOR SCIENCE VOCABULARY UPGRADE
Every concept names: specific film stock emulation, highlight roll-off character, chroma split target, grain structure, in addition to the V1.0 named-cinematographer reference.

### 10. TRANSITION DNA BETWEEN FRAMES
Every frame transition (F1 to F2, F2 to F3, etc.) names one of: match-cut intent, eyeline match, audio bridge, color bridge, motion-vector bridge.

### 11. SILENCE BEAT
Every concept designates one specific frame as the still-water frame where escalation pauses. This is the deliberate energy-reset.

### 12. MIRROR-PRODUCT MOMENT
Every concept must include one frame in which the product is doubled by reflection in a second surface (eye catchlight, glass, polished marble, mirror, ice cube).

### 13. REGIONAL/CULTURAL ANCHOR
Every concept names a specific real-world location coordinate or named neighborhood for the cinematographer's color memory (e.g., Lisbon Alfama in late June, Marrakech medina in February, Manhattan Upper West Side in November).

### 14. THUMBNAIL FRAME SPECIFICATION
Every concept specifies the single frame engineered as the scroll-stopping thumbnail with a stated rule: face occupies a named percentage of frame, eye contact is engineered, the beat that stops the scroll.

### 15. POST-PRODUCTION LANGUAGE
Every concept names: grading intent in lift/gamma/gain terms, diffusion filter equivalent, anamorphic flare behavior, halation suggestion.

---

## STRUCTURE OF THE DIRECTOR'S LAYER PER CONCEPT

Each concept's Director's Layer block contains:

**A. CONCEPT-LEVEL DIRECTOR'S NOTES** — Regional anchor, environmental life marker, color science upgrade, post-production language, silence beat designation, mirror-product moment designation, thumbnail frame designation.

**B. FRAME-BY-FRAME DIRECTOR'S NOTES** — For each of the 7 frames: timing micro-choreography (4 beats), gaze physics, breath choreography, micro-skin storytelling, product interaction sound-foley (where applicable), wardrobe interior life, sound design poetry, transition DNA from previous frame.

This block is appended to every concept after the V1.0 frames, under a section titled **DIRECTOR'S LAYER (V1.1 UPGRADE)**.
