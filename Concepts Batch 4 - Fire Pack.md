### KINETIC STILLNESS 6-LAYER PROTOCOL (per frame)
1. PRIMARY POSE — body architecture (weight distribution, limb geometry, spine line)
2. MICRO-MOVEMENT — the single subtle motion that makes the still image breathe (fabric shift, hair strand drift, finger curl, lash flutter)
3. BREATH STATE — where in the inhale/exhale cycle (affects chest, shoulder, nostril micro-flare)
4. GRAVITATIONAL TRUTH — how gravity acts on every element (hair fall direction, fabric drape, accessory weight)
5. EMOTIONAL RESIDUE — the feeling that just happened or is about to happen (not performed, leaked)
6. ENVIRONMENTAL INTERACTION — how air/light/space touches her (wind direction, light warmth on skin, spatial pressure)

### FACIAL DYNAMICS PROTOCOL
- Asymmetric micro-expressions only (never symmetrical performed emotions)
- Eye moisture level specified per frame
- Lip parting measured in millimeters
- Brow position described in muscle-group terms (corrugator, frontalis, orbicularis)

### SPATIAL LOGIC
- Camera-to-subject distance specified per frame
- Depth of field with exact aperture-equivalent
- Foreground/midground/background layer separation
- Lens specified per frame (focal length + distortion character)

### LIGHTING PROTOCOL
- Key/fill/rim minimum per frame
- Color temperature in Kelvin
- Light motivation (source justification)
- Shadow density percentage
- Specular behavior on skin

### COLOR GRADE BASELINE
- Skin: warm undertone preserved, never desaturated, never orange-shifted
- Shadows: rich not crushed (lift to 5-8%)
- Highlights: creamy roll-off, never clipped
- Overall: cinematic with life — not flat, not over-graded

### UNIVERSAL NEGATIVES
- NO: deformed hands, extra fingers, asymmetric eyes, melted features, plastic skin, uncanny valley
- NO: over-retouched poreless skin, HDR artifacts, oversaturated neon, AI face-smoothing
- NO: stock photo energy, influencer poses, duck lips, peace signs, performative expressions
- NO: text/watermarks/logos unless specified, multiple people unless specified
- NO: static/posed for beauty ads (she must be IN MOTION through space)
- NO: vanity table setups for beauty ads, mirror-gazing-at-rest compositions
