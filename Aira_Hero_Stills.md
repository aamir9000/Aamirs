# AIRA — 5 HERO STILLS · POSTER-GRADE SINGLE IMAGES (Heavy-Detail)

<aside>

- Engine: Nano Banana Pro (or equivalent) · Aspect: 9:16 vertical (4:5 safe-crop noted) · Resolution: max
- Identity: AIRA — paste character reference / face-lock here. Lock face, bone structure, hair colour and base texture to reference; zero drift. Adult, fully modest, non-sexualized. No physical/ethnic enumeration in prompts.

# STILL 1 — "COVER GIRL" (high-fashion magazine cover)

CONCEPT & USE: a bold studio cover-shot — the kind that would run as a glossy magazine front or a campaign hero. Confident, editorial, graphic, saturated. Feeling: aspirational power.
SUBJECT INTRO: Aira, [placeholder].
ANGLE & SHOT SIZE: straight-on eye-level, tight MEDIUM CLOSE-UP (chest-up), face square to camera — a true cover crop with headroom for a masthead.
WARDROBE: a structured electric-cobalt blazer worn over a high-neck black top, sharp shoulders, one bold gold statement brooch on the lapel — clean, powerful, fully modest.
FOOTWEAR: out of frame (chest-up).
EYEWEAR: none — the eyes are the cover.
ACCESSORIES: oversized matte-gold sculptural hoop earrings, a single thin gold neck-chain over the high collar; the cobalt and gold are the colour story.
HAIR: a sleek high-shine middle-part bob tucked behind the ears, glassy and architectural, not a strand loose.
MAKEUP: high-editorial — luminous skin, sharp defined brow, a glossy true-red lip (the second colour anchor), softly contoured cheek, real pores and skin texture under the gloss.
HANDS & NAILS: one hand raised to graze the jaw, fingers relaxed and elegant; glossy red nails matching the lip.
FACIAL EXPRESSION (heavy): a cool, direct, closed-lip confidence — chin level, eyes locked dead into the lens with a faint knowing arch in one brow, lips full and just-closed in a near-smirk; micro-detail: catchlights in both eyes, the faintest tension at the mouth corner suggesting she's about to speak, relaxed unfurrowed forehead, alive and present, never blank or doll-like.
BODY POSTURE & WEIGHT: shoulders squared and slightly dropped (confident, not stiff), spine tall, a subtle counter-tilt of the head — poised and grounded.
SPATIAL LOGIC: FOREGROUND — her face and raised hand. MIDGROUND — her shoulders/blazer. BACKGROUND — a flat saturated seamless studio sweep.
LIGHTING: KEY — a large soft beauty-dish slightly above, clean and even on the face. FILL — a soft reflector under the chin lifting shadows. RIM — twin kicker lights edging the hair and shoulders to pop her off the background. PRACTICAL — none. SEPARATION — bright rim vs saturated backdrop. BOUNCE — silver fill for crisp catchlights.
LENS + DOF + BOKEH: 85mm portrait, f/4 for a sharp cover (everything tack-sharp), no busy bokeh — clean and graphic.
COMPOSITION: vertical 9:16, face in the upper-centre third, generous headroom for a masthead, negative space camera-left for cover-lines; strong central symmetry.
SUBJECT FRAMING & POSITION: Aira is framed chest-up, centred and square to camera, eyes on the lens, hand at jaw. Mid-close distance, eye level. ORIENTATION LOCK — preserve this exact left-to-right composition; do not mirror, flip or invert.
ATMOSPHERE: clean, bold, expensive — a confident magazine front.
COLOUR GRADE: VIVID complementary cobalt-and-red on a saturated backdrop (try a punchy marigold-yellow or deep teal sweep) — maximum saturation, rich blacks, glossy highlights, editorial contrast.
TEXTURE & MICRO-DETAIL: skin pores and vellus fuzz under gloss, blazer wool-weave, brooch metal glint, hair high-shine, lip-gloss speculars, earring matte-gold grain.
NEGATIVE: mirrored, flipped, extra limbs, fused fingers, melted features, asymmetric eyes, garbled text, plastic skin, oversharpened, watermarks, deformed hands, dead eyes, washed-out colour, muddy grade, flat light.
CAPTION SUGGESTION: "Cover energy. No notes."

WARDROBE: an oversized slate-grey ribbed knit sweater, soft and cocooning, one shoulder slipping (modest), sleeves over the hands.
FOOTWEAR: out of frame.
EYEWEAR: none.
ACCESSORIES: minimal — tiny silver stud earrings, a thin silver ring on the hand near the face; nothing loud (the mood is the subject).

MAKEUP: bare-faced luminous — no-makeup makeup, slightly flushed nose and cheeks (cool air), glossy balm lip, real skin, faint under-eye softness that reads human and tender.
HANDS & NAILS: one sweater-paw hand resting against the window glass, fingertips touching a rain-rivulet; short clean natural nails.
FACIAL EXPRESSION (heavy): wistful, faraway calm — lips softly closed with the faintest downward rest, eyes half-lidded and unfocused on the middle distance (watching rain, not camera), brows gently relaxed, a single slow contemplative quality; micro-detail: the glass-light reflected as a cool catchlight, lashes lowered, a breath held — pensive and real, never sad-to-the-point-of-grimace, just quiet.

LENS + DOF + BOKEH: 85mm, f/2.0 — her eyes sharp, the rain on glass rendered as both sharp foreground droplets and melting bokeh, the city behind fully soft.
COMPOSITION: vertical 9:16, her face right-of-centre, rain-glass filling the left, warm lamp-bokeh upper-left — intimate negative space.
SUBJECT FRAMING & POSITION: Aira is framed in a close-up, three-quarter-to-profile, right-of-centre, gazing out past the glass. Near distance, eye level. ORIENTATION LOCK — preserve this exact left-to-right composition; do not mirror, flip or invert.

COLOUR GRADE: VIVID-but-moody — saturated teal-and-blue rain tones cut by a warm amber lamp-glow (warm/cool contrast); rich, cinematic, deep — saturated even in the grey, never flat or murky.
TEXTURE & MICRO-DETAIL: individual rain rivulets and droplets with refraction, knit-rib texture, sweater-fuzz, the temple-stuck strand, glass condensation, balm-lip sheen, skin texture.
NEGATIVE: mirrored, flipped, extra limbs, fused fingers, melted features, asymmetric eyes, garbled text, plastic skin, oversharpened, watermarks, deformed hands, dead eyes, grimace, washed-out grey, muddy flat colour.
