Title: Pillar 11 — Holographic Iridescent

Format: Fashion Film / Outfit-Change Hook Reel

Hero Product: the "Prism" — a faceted clear-crystal prism pendant on a fine silver chain worn at the throat through every change as the brand anchor, splitting the key light into tiny travelling rainbow speculars across each look (persistent continuity hero)

Wardrobe (3 LOOKS): LOOK A "Aurora Holo" — an aurora-holographic gown that shifts cyan–magenta–gold as she moves; LOOK B "Oil-Slick Iridescent" — an oil-slick iridescent mini with a rainbow-shimmer sheen; LOOK C "Pearl Prism" — a pearl-prism sculptural gown with an iridescent pearl finish

Accessories (3 + constant hero): the Prism crystal pendant worn at the throat throughout; holographic cuff + prism drop earrings (Look A); oil-slick choker + iridescent hoop earrings (Look B); pearl ear-climbers + prism-bangle stack (Look C)

Hair: — a glossy base restyled per look (a sleek holo-lit high ponytail for A, an iridescent-lit voluminous blowout for B, a sculptural pearl-lit braided crown for C); colour and face locked to reference every frame

Makeup: — holo-pop base restyled per look (aurora-holo glitter lid + glass lip A; oil-slick duochrome lid + iridescent lip B; pearl-prism lid + glossy nude lip C); glass skin throughout

Eye Colour: locked to the Aira reference — warm hazel-brown with a soft amber inner ring; zero drift, both irises matched, no heterochromia

Movement: rapid on-beat holo-shimmer / prism-shift / light-spin outfit changes, with one macro rack-focus → 3D orbit on the levitating Prism pendant (beauty mandate adapted, X.15 rule 16)

Concept Line (NOT spoken): "she shifts with the light"

Duration: 30 seconds | 6 frames

Mood: IRIDESCENT, KINETIC, LIGHT-MAXIMAL

Composition: centered hero with clean negative space tuned for each iridescent colour-world

Grade: vibrant maximal pop — saturated aurora-holo / oil-slick / pearl-prism held crisp and luminous per look, never muddy (override)

Spoken line (AR): NONE — fully silent reel, no voice-over any frame (X.15)

Audio note: diegetic sound design only per frame (no music in-clip); a separate subtle Master Track Brief sits underneath, never full

Identity safety: Aira face/bone-structure/eye colour locked to reference every frame; adult, non-sexualized; zero drift through every wardrobe change (keyframe-lock) — only clothing, footwear, accessories, hair-styling and makeup-accent change, never the face

Camera language (this concept): varied and VEO-executable across the six frames — a locked beauty push for the hook, a motivated crane drift on the holo-shimmer wash, a documentary handheld follow on the oil-slick showcase for realism, a stabilized rise following the prism-shift grow, a gimbal 3D orbit + macro rack to the Prism pendant for the finale, and a locked match-push for the loop close; handheld beats stay organic and subtle (breathing micro-sway, never jarring shake)

Visual-lead note: every change must read as a clean, magical, high-fashion swap — luminous iridescent separation and believable physics (except the one permitted magic beat), each look vibrant and pop, never muddy, murky or dull

Hook strategy: open on a pattern-interrupt tease (a held freeze + a single splitting rainbow ray), then escalate with on-beat changes (holo-shimmer wash → prism-shift grow → light snap-back), each swap a fresh visual surprise, building to a pearl-prism finale and a seamless loop back to the opening so the reel re-watches itself
