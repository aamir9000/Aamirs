# CONCEPT 84 — MAGNET-SNAP COUTURE / THREE-LOOK FLY-ON & LIGHT-ZIP POP REEL (SILENT)

Title: Pillar 7 — Pop-Art Playful

Format: Fashion Film / Outfit-Change Hook Reel

Hero Product: the "Halo" oversized chrome sculptural cuff — a mirror-finish statement wrist-cuff that stays on her wrist through every change as the brand anchor, catching and throwing each look's pop colour (persistent continuity hero)

Setting: a high-gloss pop studio — a colour-shifting seamless that re-gels to match each look (electric-lime → fuchsia → chrome-silver), a high-gloss mirror floor, clean and saturated

Wardrobe (3 LOOKS): LOOK A "Electric Lime Mod" — an electric-lime vinyl 60s mod mini-dress with crisp white go-go trim; LOOK B "Fuchsia Bloom" — a fuchsia feather-trim cocktail mini with a marabou hem; LOOK C "Chrome Mirror" — a liquid-chrome mirror-foil draped finale gown

Footwear (3): white patent go-go ankle boots (Look A); fuchsia satin platform heels (Look B); chrome mirror knee boots (Look C)

Accessories (3 + constant hero): the Halo chrome cuff worn throughout; white plastic chain-link choker + oversized white mod sunglasses pushed up (Look A); fuchsia crystal chandelier earrings + a wisp of marabou at the throat (Look B); chrome geometric collar + mirror-disc earrings (Look C)

Hair: — a glossy base restyled per look (a bouncy 60s flip for A, voluminous tousled waves for B, a sleek wet-chrome slick-back for C); colour and face locked to reference every frame

Makeup: — graphic pop base restyled per look (mod graphic liner + white inner-lid A; fuchsia smoke + glossy berry lip B; chrome-silver eye + mirror-nude lip C); glass skin throughout

Eye Colour: locked to the Aira reference — warm hazel-brown with a soft amber inner ring; zero drift, both irises matched, no heterochromia

Movement: rapid on-beat snap / unzip / mirror-spin outfit changes, with one macro rack-focus → 3D orbit on the levitating Halo cuff (beauty mandate adapted, X.15 rule 16)

Concept Line (NOT spoken): "she doesn't change clothes — they change for her"

Duration: 30 seconds | 7 frames

Mood: PLAYFUL, KINETIC, POP-MAXIMAL

Composition: centered hero with clean negative space tuned for each colour-world

Grade: vibrant maximal pop — saturated lime / fuchsia / chrome-silver held crisp and clean per look, never muddy (override)

Spoken line (AR): NONE — fully silent reel, no voice-over any frame (X.15)

Audio note: diegetic sound design only per frame (no music in-clip); a separate subtle Master Track Brief sits underneath, never full

Identity safety: Aira face/bone-structure/eye colour locked to reference every frame; adult, non-sexualized; zero drift through every wardrobe change (keyframe-lock) — only clothing, footwear, accessories, hair-styling and makeup-accent change, never the face

Visual-lead note: every change must read as a clean, magical, high-fashion swap — crisp colour separation and believable physics (except the one permitted magic beat), each look vibrant and pop, never muddy, murky or dull

Hook strategy: open on a pattern-interrupt tease (a held freeze + a finger-beckon), then escalate with on-beat changes (magnetic fly-on → light-zipper unzip-reveal → mirror snap-back), each swap a fresh visual surprise, building to a chrome finale and a seamless loop back to the opening so the reel re-watches itself
