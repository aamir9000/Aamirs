# AIRA — 5 CAPTION + HOOK PACKS (The Text Layer · Heavy-Detail)

<aside>
Five ready-to-use packs of the text that actually drives reach — the HOOK (first line / on-screen text that stops the scroll) and the CAPTION (what holds them + drives the action). Each pack is built around one content ANGLE in Aira's voice (warm, real, a little witty, never cringe). Each gives: 5 scroll-stopping hooks, a long caption, a short caption variant, CTA options, a hashtag set, and a "best for" note. Pair any pack with a Hero Still, Carousel, Spoken Script, or Reel. Aira's voice = confident but warm, self-aware, relatable; she talks WITH you, not AT you.
</aside>

## How to use
- HOOK = the first 1–2 seconds (on-screen text) or the first caption line. It must create curiosity, emotion, or a promise. Most reach is won or lost here.
- CAPTION = expands the moment, adds voice/value, and ends with ONE clear CTA + a question to drive comments.
- Rotate hooks; don't reuse the same opener back-to-back (keeps the feed feeling fresh).

# PACK 1 — CONFIDENCE / EMPOWERMENT
BEST FOR: hero stills (Cover Girl, Neon), power-walk reels, "she walked in" energy, outfit moments.
HOOKS (first line / on-screen text):
1. "Romanticize being the main character. The supporting roles are taken."
2. "I'm not for everyone. I'm for me. That's the whole point."
3. "POV: you finally stopped shrinking to make other people comfortable."
4. "The confidence wasn't given to me. I built it. Brick by brick."
5. "Walk in like the room was waiting for you. (It was.)"
LONG CAPTION: "Nobody handed me this. I used to wait for permission — to speak, to take up space, to want more. Then one day I just... stopped waiting. Confidence isn't a feeling you're born with, it's a decision you make again and again until it's yours. You don't have to feel ready. You just have to walk in. 🤍 What's one thing you're done shrinking for? Tell me below 👇"
SHORT CAPTION: "Built, not given. 🤍 Walk in like the room was waiting."
CTA OPTIONS: "Tell me below 👇" · "Save this for a day you forget your power." · "Send it to a girl who needs the reminder."
HASHTAGS: #confidence #selfworth #mainCharacterEnergy #selflove #mindset #empowerment #becomingher
NOTE: pairs best with bold, direct-to-lens visuals and a strong/triumphant audio.
