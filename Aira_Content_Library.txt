# AIRA — 5 HERO STILLS · POSTER-GRADE SINGLE IMAGES (Heavy-Detail)

<aside>
Five STANDALONE hero images — not a reel, not a chain, no animation, no transformation, no next-frame. Each is ONE image that has to be perfect on its own: a cover, a campaign key-art, an editorial portrait. Every section is engineered to the same heavy standard as the reel keyframes, but each image stands alone. 9:16 vertical (also note a 4:5 crop where useful). VIVID, saturated colour always. Aira stays adult, modest, cinematic, non-sexualized, identity-locked — likeness from reference only, never enumerated.
</aside>

## Global Spec (applies to all 5 stills)
- Engine: Nano Banana Pro (or equivalent) · Aspect: 9:16 vertical (4:5 safe-crop noted) · Resolution: max
- Identity: AIRA — paste character reference / face-lock here. Lock face, bone structure, hair colour and base texture to reference; zero drift. Adult, fully modest, non-sexualized. No physical/ethnic enumeration in prompts.
- Subject intro line for every prompt: "Aira, [placeholder]" then proceed to sections.
- Colour law: VIVID and saturated — rich blacks, glossy speculars, full mid-tone, zero murk; at least one bold saturated colour anchor per image.
- Anti-flip: every image ends on ORIENTATION LOCK — preserve the exact left-to-right composition; do not mirror, flip or invert.
- Distinctness: all 5 are deliberately different in mood, setting, palette, wardrobe and hairstyle (no two feel alike).
- Sections per still: Concept & Use · Subject Intro · Angle & Shot Size · Wardrobe · Footwear · Eyewear · Accessories · Hair · Makeup · Hands & Nails · Facial Expression (heavy) · Body Posture & Weight · Spatial Logic · Lighting (Key/Fill/Rim/Practical/Separation/Bounce) · Lens + DOF + Bokeh · Composition · Subject Framing & Position (+ Orientation Lock) · Atmosphere · Colour Grade · Texture & Micro-Detail · Negative · Caption suggestion

MAKEUP: high-editorial — luminous skin, sharp defined brow, a glossy true-red lip (the second colour anchor), softly contoured cheek, real pores and skin texture under the gloss.

SUBJECT FRAMING & POSITION: Aira is framed chest-up, centred and square to camera, eyes on the lens, hand at jaw. Mid-close distance, eye level. ORIENTATION LOCK — preserve this exact left-to-right composition; do not mirror, flip or invert.

COLOUR GRADE: VIVID complementary cobalt-and-red on a saturated backdrop (try a punchy marigold-yellow or deep teal sweep) — maximum saturation, rich blacks, glossy highlights, editorial contrast.
TEXTURE & MICRO-DETAIL: skin pores and vellus fuzz under gloss, blazer wool-weave, brooch metal glint, hair high-shine, lip-gloss speculars, earring matte-gold grain.
NEGATIVE: mirrored, flipped, extra limbs, fused fingers, melted features, asymmetric eyes, garbled text, plastic skin, oversharpened, watermarks, deformed hands, dead eyes, washed-out colour, muddy grade, flat light.

# AIRA — 5 PHOTO CAROUSELS · SWIPEABLE SETS (Heavy-Detail)

<aside>
Five carousel sets — the swipeable "photo-dump" format. Each carousel is a COHESIVE set of 6–8 stills that share one aesthetic (palette, wardrobe thread, hair, location) and tell a VIBE, not a transformation story. Slides relate by mood, not by world-state matching — no chain, no handoff. Each set gives: the cohesive look, a per-slide brief (angle/shot-size, action, key detail, palette note), a cover-slide hook, and a caption. 9:16 or 4:5. VIVID colour always. Aira adult, modest, identity-locked — likeness from reference only ("Aira, [placeholder]").
</aside>

## Global Spec
- Identity: AIRA reference-locked, adult, modest, non-sexualized, no physical enumeration. Subject intro per slide: "Aira, [placeholder]".
- Cohesion law: within a carousel, keep ONE wardrobe thread, ONE hairstyle, ONE location/palette so the set reads as one shoot. Vary only angle, shot-size, action and crop slide-to-slide.
- Distinctness law: the 5 carousels are deliberately different from each other (and from the Hero Stills) in palette, location, wardrobe and hair.
- Anti-flip: ORIENTATION LOCK on every slide — preserve the exact left-to-right composition; do not mirror, flip or invert.
- Colour law: VIVID, saturated, rich blacks, zero murk; one bold colour anchor per set.
- Detail note: each slide brief is lighter than a full hero still but still specifies angle, shot-size, action, the hero detail, light and palette so each frame is buildable.

# AIRA — 5 CAPTION + HOOK PACKS (The Text Layer · Heavy-Detail)

<aside>
Five ready-to-use packs of the text that actually drives reach — the HOOK (first line / on-screen text that stops the scroll) and the CAPTION (what holds them + drives the action). Each pack is built around one content ANGLE in Aira's voice (warm, real, a little witty, never cringe). Each gives: 5 scroll-stopping hooks, a long caption, a short caption variant, CTA options, a hashtag set, and a "best for" note. Pair any pack with a Hero Still, Carousel, Spoken Script, or Reel. Aira's voice = confident but warm, self-aware, relatable; she talks WITH you, not AT you.
</aside>

## How to use
- HOOK = the first 1–2 seconds (on-screen text) or the first caption line. It must create curiosity, emotion, or a promise. Most reach is won or lost here.
- CAPTION = expands the moment, adds voice/value, and ends with ONE clear CTA + a question to drive comments.
- Rotate hooks; don't reuse the same opener back-to-back (keeps the feed feeling fresh).
